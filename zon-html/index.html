SINCE 2023

Since its inception in 2023, GEM'S Coffee has set out to show that its coffee is the best in the business. Founded by Gemmini Collins, a pioneer in the coffee industry, and owned and operated by Eldoret natives and brothers, Paul, Steven, and Scott Ballard since date of inception, the company has stuck true to that mission and its Kenyan roots. So why is GEM'S Coffee better? Because its coffee comes from the top Arabica beans sourced from around the world, is made using superior roasting techniques in small batches and everyone at GEM'S is passionate about the art of coffee making. The diverse menu comes together with a simple goal ‚Äì to help people connect over a coffee, tea or beignet and get a distinct taste of Home!
